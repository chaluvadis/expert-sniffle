# 🚌 Local Transport Notes

## Reality in North Macedonia:

* Intercity buses exist but not frequent
* No metro system
* Taxis are common and cheap
* Rural transport is limited

### Best route:

Skopje → Ohrid by bus (~4 hours)

# 💱 Currency

## Macedonian Denar (MKD)

Approx rates:

* 1 AED ≈ 15–16 MKD
* 1 USD ≈ 55–58 MKD

Typical costs:

* Meal: 150–800 MKD
* Taxi: 100–300 MKD
* Hotel: 2000–5000 MKD/night

# ⚠️ Travel “Don’ts” in North Macedonia

* Don’t rely on strict bus timing
* Don’t expect card payments everywhere
* Don’t over-plan sightseeing
* Don’t expect fast-paced service
* Don’t travel late at night between cities
* Don’t over-negotiate small prices
* Don’t be loud in quiet places
